Classic Turkey and Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (whole wheat or your choice)
  • 2-3 slices of roasted turkey breast
  • 1-2 slices of cheese (cheddar, Swiss, or your preference)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Mayonnaise or mustard (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    • Wash and dry the lettuce leaves and tomato slices.
    • If using, spread mayonnaise or mustard on one or both slices of bread according to your preference.
  2. Layer the Sandwich:
    • Place the turkey slices evenly on one slice of bread.
    • Add the cheese slices on top of the turkey.
    • Layer lettuce leaves and tomato slices on the cheese.
    • Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Assemble the Sandwich:
    • Place the second slice of bread on top to form a sandwich.
  4. Slice and Serve:
    • Use a sharp knife to cut the sandwich diagonally or in half.
    • Serve immediately, or wrap in parchment paper or aluminum foil for later enjoyment.
  5. Optional Variations:
    • Add bacon slices for a turkey club sandwich.
    • Substitute turkey with ham, chicken, or roast beef.
    • Customize with your favorite sandwich toppings like avocado, cucumber, or pickles.
Enjoy your Classic turkey sandwich
